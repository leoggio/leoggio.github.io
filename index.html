The History of Forks

History

The word fork comes from the Latin furca,
meaning "pitchfork".Some of the earliest known
uses of forks with food occurred in Ancient Egypt,
where large forks were used as cooking utensils.
A fork, in cutlery or kitchenware, is a tool
consisting of a handle with several narrow tines
on one end.The usually metal utensil is used to lift food
to the mouth or to hold ingredients in place while
they are being cut by a knife.
